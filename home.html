About the Dev

The name's Kushaal Yadav. I'm a 18-year-old North Indian B.E-CSE student with a tech sort of background and a lot to say about the trends and direction of the contemporary design world.

I wanted to be an engineer for most of my life. By the time I changed my mind about that, my liaison with the applied sciences had drastically altered my worldview already.
